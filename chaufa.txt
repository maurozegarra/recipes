Whisk eggs together with a pinch of salt and some ground pepper. Heat 2 
tablespoons of oil in a wok or large frying pan.
Pour eggs into the hot oil and fry 1 -2 minutes, turn over and cook on the other 
side, resembling an omelet. When cooked through, place the eggs on a plate, 
coarsely chop, and reserve.

of the scallion, ginger, red pepper. Saut√© for 2 minutes. Lastly, add rice and 
chicken. Cook for three to five minutes.
